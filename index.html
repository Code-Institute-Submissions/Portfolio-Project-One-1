Our Mission!

Here at gymfyt we are committed to design and install the perfect space that completely caters to you and your individual needs. Our services are available all over the UK and overseas.

Our dedicated team work around the clock to provide you with the best care and support throughout your journey of finding your perfect workout space.

We have worked with all types of athletes from runners to boxers, football players, Olympic lifters and strongmen. We also have a great deal of experience in catering to Paralympic athletes.

Our completely customizable range of exercise equipment can be installed in almost any space, small medium or large from our free weight plated machines to multi-functioning pieces which our clients have found to be the perfect fit for smaller spaces.
